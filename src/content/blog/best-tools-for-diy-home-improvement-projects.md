Best tools for DIY home improvement projects

There is a specific kind of satisfaction that comes from fixing something with your own two hands. But as any DIYer knows, that "quick 15-minute fix" quickly turns into a weekend-long nightmare if you don't have the right tool for the job.

## The Power Trio: Drill, Driver, and Saw

Every homeowner needs a high-quality cordless drill and impact driver combo. These are the workhorses of any project, from hanging heavy mirrors to building a backyard deck.

Pair these with a versatile circular saw for making clean, straight cuts. Opt for a brushless motor if you plan on doing more than just occasional repairs, as they last much longer.

## Accuracy Tools: Levels and Measuring Tapes

Nothing ruins a project faster than a crooked shelf. A 24-inch I-beam level and a reliable 25-foot measuring tape are the unsung heroes of the toolbox.

For more advanced projects, a 360-degree laser level can save you hours of frustration. It projects a perfectly level line across all walls, making cabinetry and tile work a breeze.
